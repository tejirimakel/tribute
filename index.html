Biography

A life well lived

A devoted husband, scholar, and public servant whose towering intellect and quiet persistence touched everyone around him. This is the timeline of his journey.

  1. 1963

    Born in Rumuigbo, Port Harcourt, Rivers State.

  2. 1989

    First job as an assistant clerk at the University of Port Harcourt.

  3. 1999

    Marriage blessing with his beloved wife, Mrs. Orlu-Makele Tovo.

  4. 2005

    Promoted to faculty officer at the University.

  5. 2011

    Appointed supervisor for education in Rivers State.

  6. 2015

    Earned a Ph.D. in Sociology, then promoted to Deputy Registrar.

  7. 2016

    Passed away, leaving behind a legacy of peace and progress.
